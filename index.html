About my homeland

Joinville, Santa Catarina, Brazil

Known as the “City of Princes”, despite the constant rain, has a special place in my heart, as it is where I was born and lived for over 25 years. Located in the southern region of Brazil, it is a city with a rich history, one of the largest dance festivals in the world and the only Bolshoi dance school outside Russia.

Itajaí, Santa Catarina, Brazil

After spending about 25 incredible years in Joinville, I spread my wings and moved to Itajaí. With its bustling port, vibrant culture, and warm-hearted people, this coastal gem stole my heart. In the face of so much natural beauty, surrounded by rivers, sea and parks, I realized my dream of living on the coast. I couldn't have made a better choice, there I got married in the beautiful Igreja Matriz church, made unforgettable friends and professionally developed in one of the most innovative and recognized companies in the self-service terminal segment.
